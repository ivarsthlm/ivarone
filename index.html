IVAR.

IVAR is a pioneer in creating interactive 360-degree content and immersive Virtual Reality experiences. Our power springs from our background in documentary storytelling, with experience of telling the small story within the large perspective.

We continually create immersive narratives that hands over control to the user and results in better engagement. Our stories resonate and inspire, as they conjure the gut feeling of actually being there – as a protagonist in the middle of the story.
